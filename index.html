Select a plan or request a custom quote tailored to your hotel's unique needs and requirements. Whether you're a small hotel or a large chain, we have options that can be customized to match your price range and service goals. Based on your plan, you may be able to have more features.

2. Add Your Information

Provide us with the essential details about your hotel or hotels, including room types, amenities, services, and more. This information will help shape the chatbot’s responses and enhance its accuracy, ensuring it answers all your customers' questions correctly.

3. Integrate Into Your Website
