How It Works

Customizing your chatbot with Hotel Chat AI is simple and efficient. Follow these steps to tailor the bot to your hotel's specific needs.

1. Contact Us

Select a plan or request a custom quote tailored to your hotel's unique needs and requirements. Whether you're a small hotel or a large chain, we have options that can be customized to match your price range and service goals. Based on your plan, you may be able to have more features.

2. Wait For Us To Reply

After sending us a custome quote or selecting a plan, we will contact you back with the information you filled out as soon as we can. Then, we will be able to further compromise and talk about pricing and all the exclusive features we can offer.

3. Add Your Information

After we confirm the plan that you are on, you will need to provide us with the essential details about your hotel or hotels, including room types, amenities, services, and more. This information will help shape the chatbot’s responses and enhance its accuracy, ensuring it answers all your customers' questions correctly.

4. Customize Your Bot Further

Depending on the plan you have, you will be able to customize your bot, including the chat icon, the color of the bot, etc. Hotel Chat AI's chatbots are easily customizable to fit the style and needs of your websites.

5. Integrate Into Your Website

After you confirm that all the information you want the bot to have is correct, we will send you the single line of code that you can copy and paste into your website.

6. That's it!

Our integration is seamless and fast, ensuring no downtime during setup.
